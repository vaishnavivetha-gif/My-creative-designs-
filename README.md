## 🎨 Design Overview

The goal was to redesign a blurry, low-resolution concept into a crisp, high-impact, and aesthetically premium product poster. The design balances a bold visual hierarchy with rich color psychology to evoke warmth and morning energy.

### ✨ Key Design Elements
* **Background:** A deep, matte teal-green textured canvas with subtle vignette edges to make the central subject pop.
* **The Splash:** A photorealistic, dynamic espresso splash frozen mid-air, embedded with pristine roasted coffee beans.
* **Color Palette:** A premium contrast of rich teal, bright golden-yellow (the mug), and warm sandy-beige accents.
* **Typography:** Clean, bold white sans-serif uppercase for the main headline, combined with a lighter serif/script for the tagline.
